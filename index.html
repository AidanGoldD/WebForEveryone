Color Blindness Simulator

With around 8% of the worlwide population suffering from some sort of colorblindness, it is important to make sure that your website is accessible to those with color vision deficiencies.


Using the research of Gustavo M. Machado, Manuel M. Oliveira, and Leandro A. F. Fernandes in their study "A Physiologically-based Model for Simulation of Color Vision Deficiency" (viewable online here), you can test your sight through the eyes of those of varying colorblindness.
